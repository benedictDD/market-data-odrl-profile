The Market Data Profile for ODRL provides all the additional vocabulary and semantics required both to express market data licenses in ODRL and to automate compliance checking along the market data supply chain.

Supply Chain Metamodel

The Market Data Profile for ODRL operates within the context of the market data supply chain. The benefits of its application accrue to its actors while its definitions of compliance classify their interactions.

To unlock its potential we must situate the profile within a metamodel that describes these actors and the data resources they generate and trade.

Their interactions are also frequently of direct relevance to the validity of market data Rules.

Party Types

Party Roles

ODRL already defines the functions a Party plays in a rule. The roles here however describe the part a Party (or set of Parties) plays in the market data supply chain in the context of an Asset.

Many Parties play different roles at different times. A large financial institution plays them all.

Role

Definition:Abstract class that acts as the super-class for the concrete Roles played by Parties in the market data supply chain in relation to an Asset
Label:Role
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Role

Originator

Definition:The role a Party plays in generating/owning a Resource and acting as an Assigner of the rights and obligations controlling the use of that resource (or parts of it) by third-parties.
Scope Note:Applies to Parties deriving or curating a new creative work and acting as the Assigner of this work.
Label:Originator
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Originator
Sub-class Of:Role
In Range Of:role

Provider

Definition:The role a Party plays by distributing an Asset and acting as an Assigner of the rights and obligations controlling its use.
Scope Note:Designates the role a vendor plays in providing data to its customers.
Scope Note:Describes the role the market data function within a financial institution plays in providing data to its internal customers.
Label:Provider
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Provider
Sub-class Of:Role
In Range Of:role

Consumer

Definition:The role a party plays in receiving an asset and acting as the assignee of the rights and obligations controlling the use of that asset.
Scope Note:Designates the role a vendor or market data function within an organisation plays in receiving data from its suppliers.
Scope Note:Describes the role the end-user of market data (e.g. trading desk or quant) plays in using the data provided to it.
Label:Consumer
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Consumer
Sub-class Of:Role
In Range Of:role
