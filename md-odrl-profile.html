The Market Data Profile for ODRL provides all the additional vocabulary and semantics required both to express market data licenses in ODRL and to automate compliance checking along the market data supply chain.

Supply Chain Metamodel

The Market Data Profile for ODRL operates within the context of the market data supply chain. The benefits of its application accrue to its actors while its definitions of compliance classify their interactions.

To unlock its potential we must situate the profile within a metamodel that describes the roles these actors play and the data resources they generate and trade.

Their interactions are also frequently of direct relevance to the validity of market data Rules.

Party Types

Party Roles

Party Roles describe the part a Party (or set of Parties) plays as an Asset moves along the market data supply chain.

Many Parties play different roles at different times. A large financial institution regularly acts as an Originator, Provider, and Consumer of data assets.

In the example of an exchange delivering data to a vendor for onward distribution to a bank would look, the sequence of parties and their roles would look as follows:

The exchange acting as an Originator assigns rights and delivers data to the vendor, acting as a Consumer, which then, acting as a Provider, assigns rights and delivers data to the bank, acting as a Consumer, whose market data function, acting as a Provider, then assigns rights and delivers data to its the end users, who are the final Consumers.

And if those end-users in the bank where then to derive a new creative work from the data, they would then be acting as Originators.

Role

Definition:Abstract class that acts as the super-class for the concrete Roles played by Parties in the market data supply chain in relation to an Asset
Label:Role
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Role

Originator

Definition:The role a Party plays in generating/owning a Resource, acting as an Assigner of the rights and obligations controlling the use of that resource (or parts of it), and delivering it for use.
Scope Note:Applies to Parties deriving or curating a new creative work and acting as the Assigner of this work.
Label:Originator
Identifier:http://www.w3.org/md-odrl-profile/Originator
